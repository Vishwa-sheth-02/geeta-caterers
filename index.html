Our Story

26 Years of Passion

From a humble beginning in 2000 to being the Mumbai and Navi Mumbai's most trusted pure-veg caterer — the story of Geeta Caterers and Proprietor Mr. Amit Sheth.

The Proprietor

Mr. Amit Sheth

100% Pure Vegetarian Specialists

Geeta Caterers was founded in the year 2000 with a singular belief — that authentic, lovingly prepared vegetarian food is the finest expression of hospitality.

Under the personal guidance of Proprietor Mr. Amit Sheth, with 26 years of industry experience, the business has grown into a household name across the Mumbai and Navi Mumbai. Every menu is personally reviewed and every event personally overseen.

From weddings and corporate dinners to religious ceremonies and birthday parties — over 10,000 events catered with an unwavering commitment to freshness, tradition and excellence.
